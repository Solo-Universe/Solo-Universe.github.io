Gone with the Wind (轻错 Qīng Cuò lit. Tender Mistakes) is a Chinese music band romance by Dr. Solo. It's part of the Solo Universe — Show Biz series. The story primarily revolves around the meteoric rise and eventual fallout of the vocalist-guitarist duo of an underground rock band — West Wind.

The novel was serialized from 2018-2019 on the Chinese Web Novel website CPWXW (长佩文学网) and is completed at 99 chapters. A manhua adaptation is currently being serialized weekly on Bilibili Comics. It includes new scenes written specifically for the manhua by the novel author!

Soul Vibration (灵魂骚动 Línghún Sāodòng) tells the daily lives of the members of LOTUS, a chart-topping rock band, told from the perspective of the Bassist's personal assistant. It's part of the Solo Universe — Show Biz series.

The novel was serialized in 2017 on the Chinese Web Novel website CPWXW (长佩文学网) and is completed at 96 chapters. The manhua adaptation will debut on Bilibili Comics.

En garde Prêt Allez!! (针尖压麦芒 Zhēn Jiān Yā Mài Máng) is a fencing sports romance. The story primarily revolves around a pair of fencers that went on to compete in the Olympics.

The novel was serialized from 2016.8.1-2017.1.25 on a Chinese forum and is completed at 53 chapters. It has an audio drama and a manhua adaptation is currently being serialized weekly on Bilibili Comics.

Stargazing with You (陪你一起看星星 Péi Nǐ Yī Qǐ Kàn Xīng Xīng), also known as Samoyed and Husky (萨摩瑛与赫士奇 Sà Mó Yīng Yǔ Hè Shì Qí) is a slice of life romance by Dr. Solo. The story primarily revolves around a pair of childhood friends from kindergarten to university. It also touches on aspects of cybersecurity and white hat hackers.

The novel was serialized from 2018.3.25-2018.9.18 on the Chinese Patreon-like website AFDian (爱发电) and is completed at 37 chapters. A manhua adaptation is currently being serialized weekly on Bilibili Comics.

Lemon Lightning

秦威航

An Ning
安宁

Lemon Lightning (柠檬闪电 Níng Méng Shǎn Diàn) is a school life slice-of-life romance. This is a slow-burn of a blossoming relationship between the top student and the most attractive person in the faculty.

An Ning is a poor law student at A University. He’s nice, hardworking and has a huge sense of justice. His time is spent on studying and working to provide for himself. Qin Weihang is a handsome law student who is blunt and closed-off. He rarely attends classes and doesn’t put effort in his studies. Most of his time is spent outside of school doing the one thing he loves—rock climbing. These two with different personalities became roommates at the start of the school year and a chemical reaction waiting to happen.

The novel was serialized on 2020 on the Chinese Web Novel website CPWXW (长佩文学网) and is completed at 65 chapters. A manhua adaptation is currently being serialized weekly on Bilibili Comics.
